Q: Will my files persist if I stop my VM?
A: YES. /home is on EFS (persistent). Data survives stop/start/reboot.

Q: Can I share databases with other devs?
A: YES. Both Postgres and MySQL are shared. Each dev gets own schema.

Q: Can I disable auto-stop for 24/7 running?
A: YES. Set AutoStart: "00:00" and AutoStop: "00:00" in tags.

Q: How long does provisioning take?
A: ~5-10 minutes from Jira ticket to SSH-ready.

Q: What if I need a different OS?
A: Jira field supports RHEL8, RHEL9, Ubuntu 20.04, Ubuntu 22.04.

Q: Can I use cheaper Spot instances?
A: YES. Add SpotOptions in lambda_provision_from_jira.py for 70% savings.
